Hi, I'm James.

I code things.

A band geek at heart.

Band has been at the center of my life for the greater part of a decade. I began playing the trombone when I was in 6th grade and have continued playing it throughout high school. In 9th grade, I joined marching band and learned to play the baritone. It was around this time that I learned about drum corps: marching band on a professional level. Drum corps is a summer-long activity. You sleep, travel, and practice with the corps all summer long. Groups perform all across the country before competing at finals in Indianapolis in August. I knew it was something I wanted to be a part of. I spent two years auditioning for groups before I finally made the Blue Stars in 2020. I will be touring with them in 2021.
